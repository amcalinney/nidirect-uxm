What is Usability Testing?

Usability testing can show how easy to use something through testing it with real users. Users are asked to complete tasks, while being observed by a team of researchers, to see where they encounter problems and experience confusion. If people encounter similar problems, the research team can make recommendations to overcome these usability issues.

We use eye tracking software within our usability tests. UX researchers use eye tracking software to record users' eye behaviour when completing usability tests. This allows us to track the movement of users' pupils and generate data such as heat maps and gaze plots.


The User Experience Team available for nidirect transactional services provide usability testing in two tiers; Gold and Bronze. A summary of these can be found below.

Gold Service

  • 1 or 2 days of testing
  • Up to 8 participants per day
  • Detailed written report
  • Meeting to discuss findings

Bronze Service

  • Single day of testing
  • Maximum of 8 participants
  • Short highlight report of findings
  • Short verbal feedback

The usability testing process

The usability testing process can be split into 3 sections:

  • Preparing for usability testing
  • During the tests
  • After the tests.

We will have an initial meeting to discuss the viability of the testing. During this, we will provide guidance and clarity on anything of which you are unsure.

Preparing for usability testing

  • You will need to recruit appropriate participants and have them complete and sign a consent form.
  • Participants who have not completed the consent form will not be allowed to participate in the session.
  • You will need to ensure appropriate rooms are reserved for the duration of the tests and the afternoon prior to the first session to facilitate set-up.

During the tests

  • Participants will complete the testing one at a time in a room with one member of the user experience team.
  • Observers will be able to view testing on a screen in a separate room
  • There will be a 10 minute reset period between each participant

At least one member of your team must be present during each session. Without an appropriate observer, the testing will not go ahead.

After the tests

  • Depending on your chosen level of study, the findings report will be created. This will be delivered to you via email as a PDF within an agreed timescale.
  • If you would like further clarification on the content of the report, you can email the team or request a meeting where the findings can be presented to you verbally.

How to request the usability testing service

  • Decide if you would like the gold or bronze testing service.
  • Once you have selected which level of service you would like, you can begin the process by emailing the User Experience Team
  • We will then meet to discuss the main areas on which you would like the study to focus.
  • We will then meet again to shape these into tasks to find out this information.

If you would like to find out more about usability testing, please contact the User Experience Team and they will address any queries.
